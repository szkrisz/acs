# Business Plan: Compact Cooling System Solutions for Vegetable Cold Storage

## 🇭🇺 Hungary-Based Startup — Budapest HQ

---

## 1. Executive Summary

**Company Mission:** Deliver turnkey, compact cooling systems for vegetable cold storage facilities, featuring **proprietary control units, automation, and remote monitoring dashboard** — all as a single, plug-and-play solution.

**Business Model:**
- **Cooling hardware** → delivered by the main contractor (cooling expert + team)
- **Control system, IoT aggregators & software dashboard** → developed **in-house** (core IP)
- **Domain expertise** (special product workflows like onion) → provided by shareholder's expert technician
- **System integration, project delivery & workflow** → managed by PM/CEO

**Unique Value Proposition:** Unlike competitors who sell components separately (cooling hardware, controls, software), this company delivers a **fully integrated solution** — reducing installation time, complexity, and total cost of ownership for farmers and distributors.

**Go-to-Market:**
- **Months 1–16:** Hungary only (local pilot, references, iteration)
- **Month 17+:** European expansion (starting with neighboring countries)

## 11. Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Contractor dependency** (cooling team) | Framework agreement with penalty clauses; identify backup contractor |
| **Slow farmer adoption** | Leverage shareholder's onion industry network for warm introductions; offer "try before you buy" pilots |
| **Pályázat dependency** (customers waiting for grants) | Track active agricultural grant calls; help customers write their *pályázat* applications |
| **Single-engineer risk** (IoT/software) | CEO has IoT background as backup; document everything; modular architecture |
| **EU expansion complexity** | Start with neighboring countries with similar agriculture (SK, RO, PL); use local installer partners |
| **IP theft by contractor** | Strong NDA + IP assignment in framework agreement; control unit is a "black box" to contractor |

## 12. Legal Checklist (Hungary-Specific)

Before approaching the investor, get these documents prepared:

1. **Kft. alapító okirat** (Articles of Association) — with proper equity splits
2. **Tagi megállapodás** (Shareholder Agreement) — vesting, roles, exit terms
3. **Keretszerződés** (Framework Agreement) — with the cooling contractor
4. **Szellemi tulajdon átruházás** (IP Assignment) — for all contributors
5. **NDA / Titoktartási megállapodás** — for the contractor and expert
6. **ÁSZF** (General Terms & Conditions) — for customer contracts
